I am Chandradeep working as a Research Assistant at CVIT lab at IIIT Hyderabad working on the problem of 3D reconstruction under <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/asharmaresearch/"> Dr. Avinash Sharma </a>. Previously I have worked as a Computer Vision Engineer at <a href="http://uplara.com"> Uplara </a> working with people from MIT and IITB, on the problem of virtual try-on.

I have worked on various computer vision problems including, human-parsing, super-resolution, thin-plate spline warping, and many more.Working in a deep learning field, I have developed a sheer curiosity and research mindset towards solving new and interesting problems.

My research interests lie in the field of computer vision, applied machine learning, computer graphics, and cognition.My short term goal is to gain a significant amount of knowledge and experience to build a strong foundation. In a long run, I want to be a part of a community like AI for Earth where I can work on the problems related to climate change, global warming, agriculture, forest management, and wildlife research.
